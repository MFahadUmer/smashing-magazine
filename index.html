PERSON OF THE WEEK

Tim Wright (@iamtimwright ) has been working on the web since 2004. Since then, he has done every job from designing and coding to directing teams and organizational leadership at companies like 10up, Fresh Tilled Soil, and Boston University. Tim is passionate about teaching and giving back to the community and has shared his knowledge through writing, speaking, mentoring, podcasting , and teaching web design night classes. He has also been involved in organizing events for Global Accessibility Awareness Day and Refresh Boston. Tim loves to geek out about typography, accessibility, and web performance and writes up small tutorials on his blog . Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Tim!
